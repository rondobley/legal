Program Name

Go Hilton Rate Monitor — Personal Rate Alert Service

Description

This service monitors Go Hilton employee hotel rates and sends SMS/email alerts when rates fall below a user-configured threshold.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies. Alerts are sent only when qualifying rates are found, typically no more than a few times per day.

Message and Data Rates

Standard message and data rates may apply.

Opt-Out

To stop receiving SMS messages, reply STOP at any time. You can also remove your phone number from the application settings.
